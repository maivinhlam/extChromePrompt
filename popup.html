⚡ Flow Prompt Runner

Run your prompts on a schedule and let the extension do the work for you!

Step 1: Prepare your prompts, keeping each prompt on a new line.

Step 2: Click to the button "Import Prompt"

Step 3: Import or paste prompts into the list below.

Step 4: Choose mode and optional automation features.

Step 5: Click Start while Labs Flow is open in the active tab.
