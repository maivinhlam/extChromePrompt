Flow Prompt Runner

Run your prompts on a schedule and let the extension do the work for you!

Step 1: Prepare your prompts, keep each prompt on a new line.

Step 2: Click to the button "Import Prompts" to import your prompts into the extension.

Step 3: Paster your prompts into the textarea and click "OK".

Step 4: Click the "Start" button to start running your prompts on schedule.
